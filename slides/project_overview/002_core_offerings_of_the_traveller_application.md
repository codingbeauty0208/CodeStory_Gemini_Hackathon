### Slide 2: Core Offerings of the Traveller Application
- **Discovery**: Users can discover a treasure trove of destinations, from iconic landmarks to hidden gems, fostering immersion in local flavors and traditions.
- **Personalized Planning**: The app aims to offer a seamless and personalized travel planning experience through comprehensive guides, insider tips, and curated itineraries.
- **Community Engagement**: It encourages connection with fellow travelers, allowing them to share stories, photos, and recommendations to foster a sense of belonging and inspiration.
- **Safety & Well-being**: Provides up-to-date travel advisories and health guidelines to ensure responsible and peaceful exploration.
