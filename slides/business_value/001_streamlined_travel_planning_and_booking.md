### Slide 1: Streamlined Travel Planning and Booking
-   **User Convenience**: Offers a consolidated platform where users can plan and book multiple aspects of their travel (flights, hotels, trains) from a single interface, saving time and effort.
-   **Enhanced Accessibility**: Provides a web-accessible service, allowing users to access travel booking functionalities anytime, anywhere with internet connectivity.
-   **Reduced Complexity**: Simplifies the travel booking process by abstracting away the complexities of interacting with multiple service providers.
